Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/12/2024

EduGrades ("we", "us", or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data when you use the EduGrades application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information to ensure proper functionality of the App:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Phone number

The App is designed for users of all ages, including minors. By using the App, you consent to the collection of this data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for authentication purposes, allowing users to securely access and interact with the App. We do not use your personal information for marketing or share it for unrelated purposes.

3. Storage of Information

Your data is securely stored in Firebase, a cloud-based service provided by Google. Data is retained in our system until the user decides to modify or delete their information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations.
  • As part of Firebase's data processing services. You can review Firebase's privacy policy here.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information against unauthorized access or misuse. While we strive to ensure the security of your data, no system can guarantee absolute protection. Examples of implemented measures include encrypted data transfers and access control mechanisms.

6. Your Rights

As a user of EduGrades, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Modification: You can update your last name, first name, password directly within the App. You can ask to change your email address or phone number by sending an email to cutiips.dev@gmail.com
  • Deletion: Currently, the App does not support self-service data deletion. However, users can request to delete their data by sending an email to cutiips.dev@gmail.com. This process will be handled manually until a self-service option is implemented in a future update.

7. Children's Privacy

EduGrades takes the privacy of minors seriously. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's use of the App. If you believe we have collected personal data from a minor without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App, and the updated policy will take effect from the date of posting. We encourage users to regularly review this policy to stay informed about how we handle their data.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
